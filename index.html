About Susume Tech

Reliable backends, CI/CD & developer tooling

Susume Tech is a small software company focused on building robust backend systems, CI/CD pipelines, and developer‑friendly products.

  • Mission: To develop innovative, reliable, and user‑focused software solutions that solve real‑world challenges.
  • Vision: To be a leading South African tech innovator and the go‑to provider for trustworthy backend tooling and automated deployments for NGOs, SMEs, and local enterprises.
  • Core Values: Reliability over flash. Keep things simple and self‑hostable where it matters. Product‑first: ship small, iterate fast.
